On Wednesday, 27th of May, we will officially launch the **Holomicrobiome Institute**, a groundbreaking public-private research hub set to transform how we study and apply microbiome science.

From soil and plants to animals, food systems, and human health, the microbiome plays a critical, interconnected role in every ecosystem. The Holomicrobiome Institute is established to break down research silos, bringing together leading innovators across diverse disciplines to unlock the full power of microbiome interactions.

### The Launch Event
* **Date:** Wednesday, 27th May 2026
* **Program:** Special morning launch session featuring presentations from founding consortium partners, academic directors, and industry leaders.
* **Registration:** There are still a few spots left to join us in person for the morning program! 

Don't miss the opportunity to learn about our collaborative roadmap, state-of-the-art computational modeling, and how you can contribute to this revolutionary initiative.
