The **Dutch Microbiome Day** brings together the Dutch microbiome community shaping the future of agriculture, ecology, healthcare, and the food industry.

Join us on **11 June in Groningen** for a full day of cutting-edge research, cross-disciplinary exchange and networking.

### Event Highlights
* **Scientific Talks:** Exceptional research presentations spanning ecological, agricultural, and clinical domains.
* **Poster Sessions:** Showcase of active, upcoming, and innovative student and group project findings.
* **Walking Dinner:** Featuring curated stands from nine local food partners, showcasing the fascinating connection between soil, food, microbes, and human health.
